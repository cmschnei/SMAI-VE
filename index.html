about us

it's time to innovate as women. smaiøve is a platform by women, for women, aiming to connect social enterprises with women in tech to help develop more inclusive platforms. technology is being developed quickly, and it's important that women are not left out of the teams that are constructing the future. understanding gendered issues, caring for our lgbtqi* neighbors, and considering the unique needs of marginalized communities can only happen if the people building tomorrow's tools know what those issues are. only through constructing diverse teams will social change come about. we need to take control of our futures to make it accessible, enjoyable, and equitable for all. founders natalie çilem and katie schneider (she/her for both) seek to design a new way to approach real-world issues with gender in mind and in practice.
